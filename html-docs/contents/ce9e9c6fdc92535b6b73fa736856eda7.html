Description

Description: Given the wide variety of standards and formats in the area of digitization of content and its metadata/annotation, in order to be interoperable with major services and organizations in the field, the Digital Archives Exporting Engine component takes care of translating digital archive information (saved in IIIF format) into the most common formats (EDM, EAD etc.)

Dependencies: This component is directly connected to the digital archive
